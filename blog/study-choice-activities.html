Study choice

Published on September 10 by Valeria Cantsheva

Reasoning for choosing HZ:
The reasoning for choosing HZ was straightforward for me. I've always been fascinated by computers and technology, so pursuing a study that's focused entirely on that felt like a natural choice. HZ stood out because I know some friends who either studied or still study at HZ and really recommended the school. I knew it would be the right place for me to explore more about ICT while also gaining the practical skills I need for a successful career.

Motivate your study:
My passion for computers and technology started early, and the HBO-ICT program at HZ gives me the chance to build practical skills. I’m excited to explore my interests in ethical hacking, programming & designing while working toward a stable and fulfilling career. I don't have any programming skills as of this moment, so I want to work on that and I also want to improve my presentation skills. I tend to be quite shy at times and hope that my time at HZ will provide me with the opportunity to overcome my shyness.

Expectations after studies:
I would love to pursue a Master's in tech abroad after completing the program. I'm not sure what I want to do career-wise, it's something I still have to figure out.
